# Learnings

## MVP scope

- Level 1 is a single, deterministic raccoon raid so the day-build / night-defense loop can be tested in under a minute.
- The dog is fully automatic; player attention stays on the human homekeeping decision rather than combat controls.

## Top-down expansion

- The branch teepee is the fixed center and first upgrade anchor; the open meadow communicates future expansion without prematurely adding construction systems.
- Replays cycle through deterministic perimeter approaches, so all-angle invasion is easy to verify without adding a warning or path UI.
