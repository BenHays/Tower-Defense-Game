# Learnings

## MVP scope

- Level 1 is a single, deterministic raccoon raid so the day-build / night-defense loop can be tested in under a minute.
- The dog is fully automatic; player attention stays on a visible human build decision rather than combat controls.

## Top-down expansion

- The branch teepee is the fixed center and first upgrade anchor; four radial stick-screen sites establish outward expansion without a pathing system.
- Replays cycle north → east → south → west rather than using unseeded randomness, so all-angle defense stays easy to verify manually.
