Original Wild Hearth audio

Soundtrack preview

Four cues for the homestead’s day-to-night rhythm. Each is an original composition using a broad folk-fantasy palette of plucked strings, woodwind-like lead, soft pads, bells, and frame drum.

These tracks use general late-1990s fantasy-RPG qualities as a mood reference, but do not recreate music from any existing game.
Day / Hearth

Hearth Meadow

Warm, patient planning music for the clearing, harvesting, and construction.

Night / Watch

Forest Watch

Low strings, muted frame drum, and cautious woodwind calls for the invasion phase.

Danger

Bramble Alarm

A tighter, urgent cue for when the homestead is under pressure.

Dawn / Survived

First Light

A brief relief stinger for the moment the night ends safely.

Gameplay now plays these four tracks in this fixed order and repeats from Hearth Meadow, independent of the current game phase.
