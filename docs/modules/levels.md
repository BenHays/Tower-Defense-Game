# Levels

## Live now

- Medium uses a seeded Threat Budget that grows 25% per level, rounded up.
- Level 1 unlocks Stick Launcher.
- Holding Level 2 unlocks Potato Gun.
- Level 2 reveals Scout Training I (4 XP, +1 Scout damage); Level 3 also reveals Arrowcraft (6 XP).
- Level 4 reveals Hearthkeeping I (5 XP): at the next dawn, each surviving targetable building recovers 1 HP.
- Level 5 has a guaranteed Boar showcase. The Potato Gun has therefore been available for two levels before its counter role is required.
- Level 1 opens at a shelter site with only **Construct shelter** enabled; teepee construction is free but consumes the first full day. The first real clear/build choice begins after that tutorial action.

## Proposed next

- A compact level strip shows only the present objective or newly relevant unlock.
- Add Easy, Hard, and Very Hard only after Medium is balanced.
- Level 6 reveals Hivecraft / Bee Hive research, ahead of the Level 8 enemy family. The Mushroom path is reserved for the next family no earlier than Level 11.

## Open questions

- Exact Boar mix after its guaranteed Level 5 introduction, and the identity of the Level 8 family.

## Decisions made

- There is one fixed map. Seeds vary spawn edge, entry cell, group stagger, timing, and encounter allocation.
- Introduce at most one enemy family every three levels. Make its counter researchable and buildable at least two levels first; use a guaranteed first appearance before it enters normal random allocation.
