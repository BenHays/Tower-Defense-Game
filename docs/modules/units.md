# Units

## Live now

| Unit | Hit | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Scout | 1 base, 2 after Scout Training I | Mobile automatic final line within a day-placed watch radius. |

Scout has no player-facing health bar or upkeep system. His day-placed watch post reserves a grass cell: structures cannot overlap it, and Scout cannot be placed on a structure.

## Proposed next

- Keep friendly-unit controls out of the MVP. Scout positioning remains the only friendly placement decision.
- Future Scout upgrades use declarative Skill Point effects, calculated at combat time rather than written into saved unit state.

## Open questions

- Scout reach and tempo will be tuned against Medium outcomes after player testing.

## Decisions made

- The player is not represented by a human avatar on the map. Day actions are an abstraction.
