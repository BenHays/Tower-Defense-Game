# Terrain

## Live now

- Trees cover the map outside the central clearing. They slow enemies but are walkable.
- Clearing a tree spends the full day, grants 1 wood, and changes it into a cleared build site.
- Cleared ground is faster and can hold defenses. The original open center cannot.
- The permanent campfire is not a building or target. Its warmth zone slows nearby enemies slightly.

## Proposed next

- Add water as a terrain feature, not an inventory resource. It should enable future moisture-dependent fungal or garden structures nearby.
